10.2 Plan what happens next once you have shared or released data

It is good practice to revisit on a regular basis data that you have shared and published considering both its risk and usefulness. At the very least, you should:

  • Keep a register of all the data your share or release.
  • Consider any new shares and releases against previous shares and releases.
  • Scan the data environment by looking at such as -
    1. Other similar data released by organisations in your sector
    2. Publicly available data sources
    3. How your data is being used and by whom
