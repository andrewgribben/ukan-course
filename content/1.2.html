1.2 Describe your data situation

A data situation concerns the relationship between some data and their environments.

What do we mean by an environment?

It is in a broad sense, the context for data. It includes

  • data, and other data.
  • how people interact with that data
  • their responsibilities for it as well as the governance, and
  • the infrastructure for processing and protecting it.

What do we mean by a data situation?

A data situation describes a situation where data are moved from at least one environment to another. Sharing and releasing data can be described as data situations.
