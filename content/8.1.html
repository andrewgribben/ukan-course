8.1 Identify who your stakeholders are and plan how you will communicate

Different audiences will need different communication strategies.

How much do you want to explain of your anonymisation process? Who are you informing about your data release? Some people may want to learn about the benefits, while others care about your sound governance.

Possible stakeholders might be:

  • The data subjects, the people linked to the data
  • Funding providers
  • Users and researchers, accessing the datasets
  • The general public, who might be concerned about your data release
  • The regulators or related organisations
