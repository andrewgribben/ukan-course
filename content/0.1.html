0.1 The Anonymisation Decision-Making Framework

Anonymisation is about managing risk.

The process of anonymising data involves a balance between producing safe data and useful data. There is no such thing as zero risk but that is true in other areas of life as well. This course has been developed by the UK Anonymisation Network (UKAN) based on the Anonymisation Decision-making Framework. A central tenet of the framework is that we cannot carry out effective anonymisation by just looking at the data; the context is crucial.
