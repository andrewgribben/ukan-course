6.1 Know the processes you will need to go through to assess disclosure risk

The key question is - how might a data breach actually occur?

To assess how a data breach might occur - we recommend that you follow steps1 and 2. Step 3 is both informative and good practice but resource intensive and thus we recognise it is not an option that everyone can undertake.

The three steps are as follows:

  1. 1.Assess the data in front of you
  2. 2. Scenario analysis
  3. 3.Testing assumptions of scenarios
