0.2 The Anonymisation Decision-making Framework

The Anonymisation Decision-making Framework (ADF) has 10 components, they are:

  1. Describe your data situation

  2. Know your data

  3. The use case

  4. Understand the pre-share/release legal issues

  5. Understand the issue of consent and your ethical obligations

  6. Identify the processes you will need to assess disclosure risk

  7. Identify the processes you will need to anonymise your data

  8. Identify who your stakeholders are and plan how you will communicate

  9. Know what to do if things go wrong

  10. Plan what happens next once you have have shared or release data'
