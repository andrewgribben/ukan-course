3.2 Topic 3: The use case

Here are some ideas of how to gather potential use cases:

  • schedule an internal brainstorm, where members of the organisation can bring in their experience and expectations
  • host an external workshop, where you invite experts or users to contribute to the discussion
  • launch a formal consultation for anyone to have a say
  • collect and analyse user feedback for example via your website
  • learn from related datasets, where you can observe use cases
